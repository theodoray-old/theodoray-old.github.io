About Emmanuel


I was born in Jakarta, Indonesia. I grew up in Singapore, where I have spent most of my life studying, working and living.

I had a thing for technological hardware in my pre-teen years. My interest in the software computing world started back in 2016, when I was awed by app startup stories. Because of this, I've spent hours figuring out where to start. Tried my hand with Xcode, then Python, then Harvard's CS50. I kept getting stuck, until I decided to start off web programming with The Odin Project.

I fell in love with computer programming. If anyone wants to learn, and finds that the learning curve is too steep, I strongly recommend learning web development first. Somehow, or at least for me, it worked.

My occasional hobbies include blogging, exploring new places and eating good food.

Twenty Toes Travel Blog

Twenty Toes Travel is my pilot blog programme made from scratch with HTML/CSS/Javascript, PHP and the Wordpress CMS. This is my largest project to date, and content is updated regularly as my food & travel blog.

Maze Trotter Game

Maze Trotter is a game that I've created using MIT's Scratch. Spider-pig has to find his way through a maze to have a bite of his favourite donut. But some perils await him in the form of Homer, who tries his best to catch him.

Credit Card Verifier

This credit card verifier takes advantage of Luhn's algorithm to check whether the credit card number keyed in is valid, to VISA/Mastercard/AMEX. Programmed using the C language, this was my assignment for Harvard CS50's Problem Set 1 (2017). (Code not provided to prevent plagiarism)
