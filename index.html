About Emmanuel


I was born in Jakarta, Indonesia. I grew up in Singapore, where I have spent most of my life being accustomed to the Singaporean culture. Obviously, in this case, I love food. A lot.

My interest in the software computing world came late after my Polytechnic graduation, during my quarter-life crisis (yes, I believe that's a thing). During this time, I kept trying to figure out how to learn to code, trying my hand with Xcode first, then Python, then Harvard's CS50. I couldn't get anything, until I decided to start off with the Odin Project.

Since then, I fell in love with coding. If anyone wants to learn programming, and finds that the learning curve is too steep, I strongly recommend learning web development first. Somehow, or at least for me, it worked.

My hobbies include tennis, the occasional English football, going to new places for adventures, and cooking.
